Photos - September 2014

My daily photo project for the second month of my happiness project. Click each photo to enlarge.

  • September 1st

    I used the macro setting to capture the leaves after it rained.

  • September 2nd

    There wasn't enough light for my camera on Tuesday night so I made the shutter speed really slow and waved around the camera to create this warped effect with the light.

  • September 3rd

    This is Jane's tired face.

  • September 4th

    I had a meeting in downtown DC on Thursday. I love the angle of the building and the spacing of the cars.

  • September 5th

    I was stuck in traffic and happened to stop underneath one of those big overhead signs. From this angle it's hard to tell what it is and I think that makes it interesting.

  • September 6th

    I was excited to take pictures of the rain, but my battery was dead. I took this post-rain angled shot of my rain soaked balcony. Pleasantly surprised the plants are still alive and I love the out of focus string lights.

  • September 7th

    I took this at movie theatre by my house. From this angle it looks retro, but in reality the clock and movie theatre marquee don't really match the standard strip mall feel of the rest of the businesses on the block.

  • September 8th

    Jane is always trying to get my food. Made carrot fries for the third straight night.
